FAQ

Common Questions

NebulaNFT is a collection of 10,000 unique digital collectibles stored on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT grants access to our community DAO, staking rewards, and exclusive metaverse experiences.

Click the "Connect Wallet" button to link your Ethereum wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.). Once connected, navigate to the mint page and choose the number of NFTs you'd like to mint. Approve the transaction in your wallet and you're done!

NebulaNFT is deployed on the Ethereum mainnet as an ERC-721 smart contract. A cross-chain bridge to Polygon and other L2s is planned for Phase 3 of our roadmap.

Holders can stake their NFTs to earn $NEBULA tokens at a rate of 10 tokens per day per staked NFT. These tokens can be used for DAO voting, future mints, and exclusive merchandise.

Follow us on Twitter/X and join our Discord server for the latest updates, giveaways, and community discussions. Links are available in the footer below.
